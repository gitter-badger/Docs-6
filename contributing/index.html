Contributing to MinigameCore¶

Overview¶

What is this section about?¶

The pages in this section detail how to contribute to MinigameCore. The more contributors there are, the better MinigameCore will become.

Who should read this section?¶

The pages in this section are intended for people who wish to:

  • Volunteer to help form the MinigameCoreDocs
  • Volunteer to help develop the main MinigameCore project

Of course, you can also help by:

  • Spreading the word about MinigameCore
